Features of our powerful SMS Gateway API

Promotional SMS

Send promotional SMS like sale, special offers and discount SMS to your potential customers. Our robust SMS gateway API is developed to offer fast and secure bulk SMS services for mobile marketing services. This API allows you to send 2000 SMS per second. You can send messages to non-DND numbers from 9 am to 9 pm.

Transactional SMS

Send pre-approved transactional SMS like bank SMS, OTP SMS, password reset, alerts and account balance. You can push 10Cr messages per day through our bulk SMS gateway that is 2000 SMS per second. We have very simple documentation and quick account activation process. The gateway creates a sender id in the name of your organization and now you can send bulk SMSs 24*7.
