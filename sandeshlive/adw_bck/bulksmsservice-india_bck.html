FAQ

Frequently Ask Questions

What is Promotional SMS?

Promotional SMS used to reach mobile users, which can be used to Promote, Market and Lead generate the product or Business. As per TRAI promotional sms can be sent 9AM to 9PM and can be delivered to non DND numbers.

What is Transactional SMS?

Transactional SMS is nothing but the SMS Communication tool which can be used to communicate Alerts, Notifications, OTP’s (One Time Passwords), Greetings and so on with the registered customers. As per TRAI transactional sms can be sent 24 x 7 and can be delivered to DND numbers.
