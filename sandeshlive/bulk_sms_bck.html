Promotional and Transactional SMS

Powerful features in an elegant, straightforward API

Promotional SMS

Promotional sms generally used for different mobile marketing activity. Any discounts, offers to targeted audience. We have robust gateway to deliver process crores of messages at speed of 2000sms/second. Messages are getting delivered to non-dnd numbers and it works 9am to 9pm.

Transactional SMS

Transactional sms meant for sending to opt-in users for the communication purpose only. OTP, Password reset, account balance, alerts can be pushed through this gateway.
